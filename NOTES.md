# Notes About the RSSSF Archive


The RSSSF Archive consists of the following sections:

Current:
- Current International Tournaments   -- National Team & Clubs
- Current Domestic Results    --   Club Leagues & Cups by Country

Historical:
- International Club Results
- International Country Results
- Historical Domestic Results

Misc:
- Miscellaneous

(via <https://www.rsssf.org/archive.html>)
